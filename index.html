The Opportunity

Nothing Else

There are properties, and then there are legacies. Ten Thunder Road is the latter — a 6,200-square-foot contemporary ranch that commands one of the most coveted positions in all of Canyon Mesa Country Club, where panoramic views of Bell Rock, Courthouse Butte, and the Tonto National Forest unfold from every level. What was built in 1994 was entirely reimagined in 2017, the result of an uncompromising commitment to craft that touches every surface, system, and space within.

The estate spans three distinct levels of living, each with its own purpose and character. Vaulted ceilings with exposed wood beams carry through the main residence, while a grand double-height entry foyer with a sculptural spiral staircase announces the scale of what lies beyond. The primary suite opens directly to the pool deck. A private theater with wet bar, a dedicated fitness room, and a mother-in-law suite with its own entrance and garage ensure that every need — for solitude or celebration — has its proper place.

The compound extends beyond the residence: a 7-car garage including two RV bays, a 21kW solar system, and short-term rental zoning that opens the door to compelling income potential. Canyon Mesa's HOA covers exterior maintenance, unlimited golf, tennis, and pickleball — a lifestyle without compromise, a holding without equal.

"Reimagined for a new generation of Sedona living."

The Location

Canyon Mesa
Country Club

Set within one of Arizona's most celebrated executive golf communities, 10 Thunder Road occupies a premier position where the golf course meets the red rock wilderness. Canyon Mesa is not merely a neighborhood — it is a curated lifestyle, set against a backdrop that has no peer in the American Southwest.

The HOA, at $2,028 per month, is perhaps the most compelling value proposition in Sedona: it covers all exterior maintenance and grants unlimited access to golf, tennis, and pickleball — a complete resort experience woven into the fabric of daily life. Beyond the gates, the Village of Oak Creek and the Red Rock Scenic Byway are moments away. Tonto National Forest trails begin at the perimeter. Sedona's acclaimed restaurants, art galleries, and cultural institutions are a fifteen-minute drive north.
